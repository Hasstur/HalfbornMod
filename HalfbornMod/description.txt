Currently this mod is in alpha stage, so any bugs could be possible.

Halfborn Mod presents terraria players a brand new way to fight enemies - in the monster form. Custom transformation allows you to turn into a mighty demon with powerful abilities. Also, the Universal Item helps prehardmode mages a lot. Enjoy!

Mod adds:
+61 items
+4 buffs 
+7 NPCs 
+14 tiles

PS: special thanks to Kostya Treskov for providing some sprites and demon pixel concepts.
